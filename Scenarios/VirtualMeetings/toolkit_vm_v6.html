Virtual Meetings

Quick Reference Guide

Whether you need a quick chat with a colleague or an enterprise-wide town hall, J&J has the right tool for the job. To help you decide, we've organized our virtual meeting tools by level from our core tool (Microsoft Teams) all the way up to large events (Zoom Webinars).

Use the table below to determine which tool best suits your needs and click the tool to see how to get started. If you need further support, Online Event Consulting and Production Company Support Services are available.
